AppHeader + ScreenHeader coexistence
The AppHeader is the outermost chrome — brand left, actions right. Below it, each sub-page renders its own ScreenHeader with a back button and page title. They share the same 1200px content band so their left edges (logo / back button) sit on the same invisible horizontal rail.

1200px content band alignment
